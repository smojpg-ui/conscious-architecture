# Ethics of Use

## Founding Principle

**We aid the operator. We do not optimize the operation.**

Consciousness Architecture and the Persistent Operational Twin System (POTS) are designed to strengthen the agency, health, and continuity of the individual operator — astronaut, analog crew member, worker, patient, user.

## What This Framework Is Built For

- Reducing self-report burden on crew in isolated, confined, extreme environments
- Preserving institutional memory across rotation boundaries in multi-vendor bases
- Surfacing behavioral health patterns to the operator themselves, for their own benefit
- Supporting the continuity of individualized care in environments where traditional medical assistance is delayed or unavailable

## What This Framework Is Not Built For

Consciousness Architecture and POTS will not be licensed or deployed for:

- Weapon targeting or lethal autonomous systems
- Coercion, interrogation, or covert surveillance of individuals without their consent
- Workforce or crew compliance enforcement
- Pre-emptive risk scoring that penalizes individuals
- Any deployment where the operator cannot exit the system, correct their twin, or delete their data

## Edge Sovereignty Is Non-Negotiable

The twin belongs to the operator. The operator owns it, controls access to it, and can delete it at any time. The architecture runs locally on operator-controlled hardware. Only lightweight, anonymized model updates (where applicable) leave the edge.

This is a required condition of use, not a design preference.

## Licensing

All commercial licensing and deployment agreements carry these principles as enforceable terms. Operators and institutions licensing POTS agree to the above as conditions of deployment.
